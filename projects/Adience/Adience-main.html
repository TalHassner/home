This webpage provides resources for the joint research performed by the Computer Vision lab at the Open University of Israel (OUI) and Adience on analysis of face images. 

Our goal is to provide open research resources reflecting the challenges of modern face recognition and processing tasks. These include comprehensive and challenging face image collections as well as code for alignment and recognition.
